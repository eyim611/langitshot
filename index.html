About Us

We Shoot From Above

Based in Kuantan, Pahang, LangitShot delivers cinematic aerial photography and videography using the DJI Mini 5 Pro — one of the most capable compact drones available today.

From the glittering KL skyline to the lush paddy fields of Pahang, we capture perspectives that ground-level cameras simply cannot reach. Every shot is carefully composed, graded, and delivered to tell your story from a new angle.

Whether you need property listings, event coverage, or creative content — LangitShot is ready to fly.
